WOOLSEY HALL

Welcome to Woolsey Hall, the university’s main concert hall. Here, you can catch performances by groups such as the Yale Symphony Orchestra, the Yale Concert Band, and the Yale Glee Club. Right now, you are listening to our orchestra performing “Amazing Grace.” Did you know that Grace Hopper was called “Amazing Grace” because of her high naval rank and many accomplishments in STEM? She was truly incredible!
