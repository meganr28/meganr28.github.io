Mini Minecraft

December 2021

Description: An implementation of the popular 3D world exploration game Minecraft and based on the TV series Avatar: The Last Airbender. This was a group project with two other students.

My Contributions:

  • Procedural terrain
  • Mountain, grassland, island, and tundra biomes
  • Multithreaded terrain generation
  • Procedural sky
  • Procedural assets (crystals, boats, trees)
  • Lava and green crystal placement (glow implemented by teammate)
