Katara Character Modeling

Description: A model of the character Katara from Avatar: The Last Airbender. I began the modeling process by creating a base shape for the head, and then modifying that to follow Katara’s features. I modeled the body by extruding from the head geometry and then duplicated and offset the body geometry to create clothing.

Programs: Maya, Arnold
