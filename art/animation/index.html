1. Animated Short Film: “Monsoon”

December 17, 2019

VIEW PROJECT VIDEO HIGHLIGHTS

University of Virginia ARCH 5420 Final Project

Description: I created a 1-3 minute animation incorporating themes from the entire semester (time, light, etc.).

Features and Responsibilities:

  • Modeled, shaded, rigged, and animated all characters and settings (with the exception of the gate and power line - courtesy of TurboSquid)
  • MEL scripting to manipulate the rain, wind, and light intensity
  • Rendered over 3,500 frames in V-Ray using multiple computers
  • Features V-Ray Mesh Lighting and Environmental Fog

Improvements:

  • Improve the pacing of the ending
  • Character movement - make walking and grabbing movements smoother (perhaps use motion capture or a walk cycle)

Fun Fact: Based on a coconut vendor I see when I visit my family in India.
