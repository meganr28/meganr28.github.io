About Me

I am a master’s student at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing an MSE in Computer Graphics and Game Technology (exp. May 2023). I received my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Virginia in May 2021. After taking an animation class in UVA’s architecture school, I developed a strong interest in computer graphics and animation. Luckily, computer science gave me the flexibility to explore the intersection between mathematics, art, and programming and use them to develop tools for artists.

Graphics Interests

My current interests include rendering and shading, GPU architecture, research and development, and tool development. During my graduate studies, I would like to continue learning about rendering techniques and explore other areas of the field such as image processing and machine learning.
